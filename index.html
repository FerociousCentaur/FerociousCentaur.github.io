Latest projects

I am really interested towards open-source contribution and have created few packages

Slide Extractor

It is a python package to extract slides and presentations from videos. I created it to make lives of college students a bit easier. Understanding the fact that its not possible to attend every online lecture, and rewatching them just to take screenshots of lecture slides is too painfull. Thus, this package aims at extracting slides from video lectures.

Billdesk Integration Kit

It is a basic kit for integration of billdesk payment gateway in your webapps. It has all the functionalities inbuilt like checksum generation and validation, data extraction, data generation in the required format and much more. It's integration kit is available for both Django and Nodejs and is available on pypi by the name of "django-billdesk" and on npm by "billdeskjs".
